About the hotel

Set on 2 private islands connected by a walkway and surrounded by the Indian Ocean, this luxury resort is a 30-minute seaplane ride from Velana International Airport in Male. Bright, chic villas come with flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, and coffeemakers. Some have a private garden, an open-air bathroom and/or direct access to the beach. The higher category villas are additionally equipped with a solarium with hot tub, private swimming pool and/or glass floor. A few have a personal butler and/or a hydrotherapy treatment room.
